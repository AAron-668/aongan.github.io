Our Beeswax Bread Bags

Natural, Reusable & Eco-Friendly

AONGAN Beeswax Bread Bags are designed for baking lovers and eco-conscious families. Made with natural materials, they are reusable, breathable and perfect for storing all kinds of bread—sourdough, artisan bread, rolls, baguettes and more.

Core Features

  • 100% Natural Materials: Beeswax, organic cotton, jojoba oil—safe and non-toxic
  • Reusable & Eco-Friendly: Replace disposable plastic bags, reduce waste
  • Fresh-Keeping: Breathable barrier keeps bread soft inside, crispy outside
  • Easy to Use & Care: Hand washable, reusable for 6–12 months with proper care
  • Stylish Design: Minimalist, natural style, perfect for home and picnic
