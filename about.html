About AONGAN

AONGAN is dedicated to providing natural, reusable, and eco-friendly kitchen supplies for baking lovers and families who pursue a healthy lifestyle.

We focus on beeswax food storage solutions, replacing disposable plastic bags with safe, breathable, and long-lasting beeswax bread bags.

All our products are made of pure natural beeswax, organic cotton and jojoba oil, without any harmful chemicals. Safe for bread, fruit and daily food storage.

Our mission is simple: Protect your food freshness, protect your family health, and protect our planet from plastic waste.
