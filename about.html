About AONGAN

Nature · Health · Eco-Friendly Living

Our Story

AONGAN was born from a simple idea: to create safe, natural and reusable food storage tools for every family who loves homemade baking.

We are deeply aware of the harm caused by disposable plastic bags to human health and the environment. So we focus on developing premium beeswax bread bags as a perfect plastic-free alternative.

From material selection to handmade craft, we stick to high standards to bring healthy and eco-friendly daily necessities to global users.

Pure Natural Material

We only use pure natural beeswax, organic cotton fabric and jojoba oil, without any synthetic chemical additives.

The material is safe enough to directly contact bread, fruits and other foods, breathable and fresh-keeping, allowing you to taste the original flavor of food.

Every AONGAN beeswax bag is carefully made to balance beauty, practicability and environmental protection.

Our Mission & Vision

We hope to let more families stay away from disposable plastic and embrace a low-carbon, green and healthy lifestyle.

Not only providing high-quality beeswax bread bags, but also spreading the concept of environmental protection and healthy life to every corner of the world.

Choose AONGAN, choose nature, choose a better life for you and the planet.
